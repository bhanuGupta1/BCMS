
Book Collection Management System (BCMS)

>Overview

The *Book Collection Management System (BCMS) is a web-based application designed to help users efficiently manage and organize their personal book collections. This system allows users to add, update, delete, and search for books within their collection, providing an intuitive interface for better book management. The application also offers features to categorize books, track reading progress, and maintain details about book friends.

>Features

- Book Management: Add, update, delete, and search books within the collection.
- Reading Progress Tracking: Monitor books currently being read and those already completed.
- Categorization: Organize books by genre, author, or other criteria.
- Friend Management: Manage a list of friends associated with your books.
- Collection Statistics: Get insights into your reading habits and book collection.


>Usage

- Add a Book: Use the "Add Book" form to add new books to your collection.
- Update or Delete Books: Manage your books using the options provided in the book list.
- Search Books: Use the search functionality to quickly find books in your collection.
- Track Reading Progress: Update the reading status of books to keep track of your progress.
- Manage Friends: Add or remove friends associated with your book collection.
